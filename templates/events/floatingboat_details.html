Boat On

A boat is to prepare with concrete and admixture and the analysis will be done by putting weight in boat and it should not sink

Venue: Concrete Lab (36)

Participation: 11:00 am - 1:00 pm

Eligibility: Any Students from Civil department having valid id is eligible to participate in the event.

Register Now

Rules and Regulations

  • The entry in Boat-on competition is open to all civil engineering students of Chandigarh Engineering College.
  • Minimum 3 and maximum 5 students are allowed in each group of Boat-on competition.
  • Material such as Cement, Coarse aggregate and Equipment will be provided.
  • All other material required has to be carried out by student only.
  • To be eligible to win the prize, you must meet the following criteria at the end of the competition period.
    • A boat is to prepare with concrete and admixture and the analysis will be done by putting weight in boat and it should not sink.
    • Setting time of 3 hrs will be given to set concrete.
    • Boat who will retain more weight is the winner.
  • In-situ construction will be allowed. Nothing Pre-prepared material will be used.
  • The promoters will announce the result on Engineers day.
  • Wearing student I card is must.
