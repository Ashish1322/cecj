Rules and Regulations

  • Themes will be provided to all Teams. The Team needs to pick up one Theme and build upon it.
  • The solution can be related to any domain shared.
  • Team leaders must create a common Git-Hub repository and all other Team-Members will push their code on that only.
  • All members of the Team must share their Git-Hub profile link with us as well as Team leaders must share their project repository.
  • Teams can take help from the internet if they are stuck at some point but they must use their own creativity and skills to develop a unique solution.
  • Teams can use any framework or library at their convenience.
  • Plagiarism is strictly prohibited; the solution of all Teams will be strictly monitored and if found to be copied from other teams or a complete solution from the internet, then theyâ€™ll be immediately Disqualified.
  • After completion of the project, it must be live (Must be hosted on the internet, can use GitHub Pages or Heroku or any free platform).
  • Teams must also create a PPT and Report of their projects & will need to present their project along with the PPT on the day of final judgement.
  • Teams must update their status of projects every 3 hrs in a common WhatsApp group, If they fail to do so they will be contacted by Event Coordinators, If any Team remains inactive for more than 6 hrs, then the team will be disqualified.

Minute to Minute for Event:

  • All Teams must share a Description of their Project Idea in a Form shared with them by 03:00 PM, 10th September 2021.
  • After the Welcome Meet of the event, Teams will start working on their project idea by 12:00 PM 13th September 2021 and All members of Teams must share their GitHub Profiles. Also, Team Leader must share the link to the repository of their project by 12:00 PM.
  • All Teams must share their project status every 3 Hours from 10:00 AM to 8:00 PM on 13 and 14 September 2021.
  • The first judging round of projects will be held on 14 September 2021 (Morning), and updates or improvements will be notified on the same day (afternoon).
  • The final Judgement round will be held on 15th September 2021 (Noon).
  • Teams will present their projects with live demonstrations during the Final Judgement Round on 15 September 2021.
  • Results will be declared in a meeting by 4:00 PM on 15 September 2021.
