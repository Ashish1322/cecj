Mech Relay

This event is like a relay game but it consist of some questions which has to be answered while playing and it makes this more intersting.

Venue: Ground(Front of Block I)

Timings: 11:30 am onwards

Eligibility: Any Students from Mechanical Engineering Department having valid Id card can participate.

Register Now

Rules and Regulations

  • Each Team must contain four members.
  • Runner has to give chance to another participant after completing his/her questions.
  • Participants must wear black trouser and white t-shirts.
  • Runner must remain in their lane throughout the race.
  • Runner must not obstruct or block another runner. If they do so they will be disqualified.
  • Runner must not contain any type of sheet or paper in his/her pockets.
  • Distance between two desks is 20m.
