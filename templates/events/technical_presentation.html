Technical Presentation

Technical presentation is an individual competition where each participant is required to make a Power point presentation about a given topic or area, and then present it in front of jury member by following all the rules and regulations.

Venue: Seminar Hall ( Block 1 )

Timings: 10:30 am onwards

Eligibility: Any student with valid Id Card.

Register Now

Rules and Regulations

  • The participant will get 07 minutes for presentation followed by question and answer session.
  • It is advisable that the presentation focuses on one particular topic. It should be start up idea, Innovation or upcoming technology topics.
  • The participants who are desirous of choosing a different theme than those mentioned are welcome, provided, it falls under the domain Kindly contact the coordinator for clarifications.
  • Bring your college I-Card & receipt on event day.
  • Please bring your Interactive Power Point presentation on a CD and on a pen-drive.
  • The registration with submissions should contain:
    • Title- Theme of the Presentation.
    • Name of the author
    • Phone no of author
    • E- mail ID.
    .
  • Presentation must contain index, introduction, point wise description of subject and conclusion and references.
  • Last day to submit your presentation (via e-mail to faculty coordinator) will be two days prior to the day of presentation.
  • The decision of judges will be final and no arguments or appeal will be entertained.
