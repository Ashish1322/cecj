Technical Presentation

Description

Technical presentation is an individual competition where each participant is required to make a Power point presentation about a given topic or area, and then present it in front of jury members. The Topic of the presentation will be related to Technical aspects.

Mode: Online

Participation: Individual

Eligibility: Students having a valid id card of any branch are eligible to participate in this event.

Register Now

Rules and Regulations

  • Anyone can participate individually.
  • Duration of 6 min will be given for the presentation.
  • Topics for presentation are AI, Data Science, ML, IOT ,Mechatronics, Cyber security, Block chain , 5G technology, Robotic process automation (RPA), Hybrid Cloud, Internet of Behaviours(IOB) and any other latest advancement in technology.
  • Presentation should be neat and clear to all the audience.
  • Winners from each stream may be announced
  • The certificate of participation will be provided to all the participants.
