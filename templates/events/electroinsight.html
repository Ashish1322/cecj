Arduino and Raspberry Pi Challenge

Description

An Arduino coding and circuit designing Challenge. Design of sensory systems using the platform Arduino by undergraduate coding and basic knowledge of electric circuits and show your skills.

Mode: online

Participation: Maximum 2 members

Eligibility: Students pursuing B.Tech in Electronics and Communication/Electrical or any allied branch are eligible to participate in this event.

Register Now

Rules and Regulations

  • The students have to participate in teams. There should NOT be more than 2 members per team.
  • The teams have to build such a project that includes : Arduino board or Raspberry Pi board
  • Students will have to assemble and code their project within 3 hrs of the given time. Already built projects will NOT be accepted.
  • Students should have documents that contain all the details about their project.
  • The criteria of the evaluation will be: Documnetation and Explanation, Complete and Functional, Creativity, Understanding, Applications.
  • The certificate of participation will be provided to all the participants

Preferred Themes.

NOTE: These are only preferable topics, participating students are however free to choose any technology/theme of their choice.

  • HealthCare Sector and its applications
  • Agriculture Sector and its applications
  • Community Service related applications
