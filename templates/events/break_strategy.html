MCQ, Coding Competition based on different programming languages consist of 2 Rounds. Let’s venture together and have fun while coding.

Rules and Regulations

  • This is going to be a 2 round event consisting of an MCQ round and a coding round.
  • 1st round will be held on Google Form Platform and It will consist of 20 Questions of the Easy to Medium difficulty level.
  • After Successfully Attempting round 1 participants will be provided with a link to Round 2.
  • Round 2 will be a coding round that will be held on the Hackerrank platform and It will consist of 4 interview level Questions with a difficulty level ranging from Medium to Hard.
  • lagiarism is Strictly prohibited if found it will lead to disqualification of the participant.
  • Participants must attempt both Round 1 as well as Round 2, then only they’ll be eligible for winning criteria and participation certificate.
  • Link to Round 2 will be provided at the submission section of Round 1 Quiz. So participants must notice that.
  • Ensure your network connectivity during the event as we will not be responsible for any of the inconvenience you suffer during submission

JUDGING CRITERIA

  • Effective And Best Algorithm
  • TimeSpan
  • Concise Solution to a Problem.
  • IN CASE OF A TIE IN ROUND 2 SCORE, THEN PARTICIPANTS HAVING A HIGHER SCORE IN ROUND 1 WILL BE SELECTED AS WINNERS.
