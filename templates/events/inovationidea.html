CECJ-INNOVATION IDEA

Description

Students will have to prepare an idea on the basis of different type of themes and Students should have to prepare a ppt that contains all the details about their project.

Mode: Online

Participation: Maximum 2 members

Eligibility: Any Students having valid id card is eligible to participate in this event.

Register Now

Rules and Regulations

  • Students can participate individually or in teams of 2.
  • The students have to put forward the business idea.
  • Students will have to prepare an idea
  • Students should have a ppt that contains all the details about their project.
  • Members should present their project details and innovative ideas through PPTs and Demonstration.
  • The teams will be evaluated by the jury panel on the basis of DOCUMENTATION AND EXPLANATION, CREATIVITY, UNDERSTANDING, APPLICATION
  • THE CERTIFICATE OF PARTICIPATION WILL BE PROVIDED TO ALL THE PARTICIPANTS.

Following themes should be preferred:

    Note: These are only preferable topics, participating students are however free to choose any technology/theme related to society issues.

  • HealthCare Sector and its applications.
  • Agriculture Sector and its applications.
  • Community Services related applications.
