B-Plan Competetion(Start up Ideas)

Let's have fun with colors and make beautiful rangoli. Play with colors and create some amazing designs.

Rules and Regulations

  • Teams of two participants are allowed for this event.
  • Students have to present any Start-up idea through PowerPoint Presentation.
  • Time limit for each team will be 5-10 minutes.
  • A specific name to be given for a Start-up Idea by the team.
  • Any Start-up idea by the team should broadly address the problem targeted, how it would be resolved, who all will be benefit and how you will reach the customer; any prototype of Minimum Viable Product could also be displayed.
  • Mode of Language should be English only.
  • Students should present the original idea.
